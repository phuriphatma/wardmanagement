          Bring a ward list in from a Google Sheet in three quick steps.
          

    
    
          
      
          Step 1 · Paste your sheet link
          
      
          
        How do I get the link?
        
            
          
          1. In Google Sheets, open File → Share → Publish to web.
            2. 
          
          2. Choose the tab (or “Entire document”) and pick Comma-separated values (.csv).
            3. 
          
          3. Click Publish, then copy the link it gives you and paste it below.
            4. 
        
          
      
          
      
      
      
          
        
        
      
          
    
          

    
    
          
      
          Step 2 · Choose a tab (only if your sheet has more than one)
          
      
      
          Type the tab’s name and press Enter, or just click one below.
          
      
          
        
          Tabs in this sheet — tap one to load it:
          
        
          
      
          
    
          

    
    
          
      
          Step 3 · Match your columns
          
      
          Tell the app which column holds what. Use the dropdowns, or press Pick and then tap a column in the preview below.
          
      
          
        
        
      
          
      
          
        
        
      
          
      
          
        
        
          “active / admit” → Active · “discharge / dc / d/c” → Discharge.
          
      
          
      
          
        
        
          Hospital number / MRN, if your sheet has one.
